Most businesses extract value. They take attention, money, and resources. Regenerative businesses do the opposite: they create more value than they consume, leaving systems better than they found them.

The regenerative ladder moves from sustainable to restorative to generative. Each rung increases your positive impact on the world.

Beyond Sustainability

Sustainability maintains. Regeneration improves:

  • Sustainable: Don't harm the environment
  • Regenerative: Improve the environment
  • Sustainable: Treat workers fairly
  • Regenerative: Help workers thrive
  • Sustainable: Give back to community
  • Regenerative: Community co-creates with you
Approach Outcome
Extractive Takes more than gives
Sustainable Takes and gives equally
Regenerative Gives more than takes

Regenerating Your Community

How your ladder can give back to your audience:

  • Free education that genuinely transforms
  • Community that supports members
  • Platform for members to share their voices
  • Economic opportunities for your community
  • Recognition and celebration of members

Regenerating Your Industry

Contribute to your field:

  • Share what you learn, even proprietary insights
  • Mentor emerging creators
  • Collaborate rather than compete
  • Raise standards in your niche
  • Advocate for ethical practices

Regenerating the Planet

Consider environmental impact:

  • Offset your carbon footprint
  • Support environmental causes
  • Model sustainable practices
  • Create content about environmental issues
  • Partner with regenerative organizations

Regenerative Business Models

Structure your business to give back:

  • Percentage of profits to causes
  • Buy-one-give-one programs
  • Community ownership or profit-sharing
  • Scholarships for those who can't afford
  • Investing in community projects

The Abundance Mindset

Regeneration comes from believing there's enough:

  • Success doesn't require others to fail
  • Giving doesn't diminish what you have
  • Collaboration creates more than competition
  • Value can be created infinitely
  • Your success can lift others

Consider how your ladder can become regenerative. Where can you give more than you take? What would it mean for your community, industry, and planet to be better because of your presence? Build not just to sustain, but to regenerate.
