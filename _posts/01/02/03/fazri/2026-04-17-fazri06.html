Nonprofits have a different currency: mission impact. You're not selling products; you're inviting support for a cause. The value ladder still applies, but the rungs are different.

The nonprofit ladder moves supporters from awareness to action to ongoing engagement. Each interaction leaks your mission and impact.

Awareness: Leaking Your Mission

Share stories of your impact. Introduce the people you serve. Explain the problem you're solving. These leaks build awareness and emotional connection.

Focus on specific, relatable stories, not abstract statistics. One person's story creates more connection than a thousand numbers.

Stage Content
Awareness Impact stories, mission
Engagement Ways to help, events

First Action: Low-Barrier Engagement

Make it easy to take first action. Sign a petition. Share a post. Attend a free event. These low-barrier actions build momentum toward deeper involvement.

First Donation

The first donation is a milestone. Make the process easy and emotionally rewarding. Show exactly how their gift will help. Thank them personally.

Ongoing Giving

Monthly donations provide sustainable funding. Show donors the cumulative impact of ongoing support. Make upgrading to monthly easy.

Major Donors and Legacy Giving

At the top, major donors and legacy gifts. Cultivate these relationships personally. Show the transformational impact of significant support. Honor their commitment.

If you work with a nonprofit, map your supporter journey against this ladder. What's the next step for each segment? How can you better move supporters up?
