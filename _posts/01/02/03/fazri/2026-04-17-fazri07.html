Ghostwriting is unique: your work appears under someone else's name. Your success depends on capturing their voice while delivering value. The relationship is intimate and trust-based.

The ghostwriter ladder moves clients from single pieces to ongoing retainers to exclusive partnerships. Each project builds understanding of their voice and needs.

One-Off Projects

Single articles, blog posts, or speeches. These let clients sample your work with minimal commitment. Deliver exceptional quality that captures their voice.

The goal is earning the next project.

Project Goal
One-off Demonstrate fit
Retainer Ongoing relationship

Retainer Relationships

Monthly retainers provide predictable income. You handle all their content: blog posts, LinkedIn articles, newsletters. You become their external voice.

Exclusive Partnership

At the top, you're the exclusive writer for key clients. They trust you completely. You may be involved in strategy, not just writing. These relationships are golden.

Building Your Portfolio

Ghostwriting portfolios are tricky. Use anonymous samples or get permission to share with attribution. Focus on results: client growth, engagement, opportunities.

If you ghostwrite, assess your client relationships. Which could become retainers? What would it take to move them up? Start conversations about ongoing work.
