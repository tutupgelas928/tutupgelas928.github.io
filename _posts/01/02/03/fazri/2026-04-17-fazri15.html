Virtual assistants provide essential support to busy professionals. The relationship often starts with simple tasks and can grow into strategic partnership.

The VA ladder moves from hourly work to packaged services to strategic retainer. Each rung increases value and deepens trust.

Hourly Task Work

Start with hourly work on specific tasks. This lets clients test your reliability with minimal commitment. Deliver exceptional quality and communication.

Level Structure
Hourly Per-task billing
Packages Fixed hours/month

Hourly Packages

Offer monthly packages of hours. Clients get predictable billing; you get predictable income. Packages encourage clients to use all their hours.

Service Packages

Package specific services at fixed prices. "Social Media Management Package" or "Inbox Zero Package." These are easier to sell than open-ended hours.

Strategic Retainer

At the top, you're a strategic partner, not just task-doer. You anticipate needs, suggest improvements, and think proactively. This level commands highest rates and deepest relationships.

If you're a VA, review your service offerings. What packages could you create? What would move clients to higher rungs? Develop one new package this quarter.
