Membership sites offer recurring revenue and deep relationships. Members pay monthly for ongoing value. This model rewards consistency and community.

The membership ladder moves from free content to paid membership to premium tiers. Each month, you leak value that justifies continued subscription.

Content Calendar for Consistency

Members pay for ongoing value. Plan your content calendar monthly. Include:

  • Regular training or lessons
  • Live Q&A sessions
  • Community discussions
  • Resource updates
  • Guest expert appearances

Consistency builds the habit of engagement.

Content Type Frequency
Training Weekly
Live Q&A Monthly

Community as Retention Driver

Members who connect with each other stay longer. Foster community through:

  • Discussion prompts
  • Member spotlights
  • Networking opportunities
  • Collaborative challenges

Acquisition Through Leaks

Attract new members by leaking membership content publicly. Share excerpts, host free events, offer previews. Give prospects a taste of membership value.

Tiered Membership

Offer multiple tiers at different price points. Basic tier gets core content. Premium tier adds live access, coaching, or other benefits. Tiers let members choose their level.

Managing Churn

Members will leave. Survey them to understand why. Use exit interviews to improve. Welcome back lapsed members with win-back campaigns.

If you run or plan a membership, map your content calendar and community strategy. What keeps members engaged? What would reduce churn? Implement one improvement this quarter.
