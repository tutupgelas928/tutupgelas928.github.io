The SEO ladder moves from audit to implementation to ongoing optimization. Each rung demonstrates value and deepens engagement.

SEO Audit

The audit is your entry point. Analyze their current situation, identify issues, and provide recommendations. Deliver genuine value while showing what's possible.

Level Focus
Audit Analysis, recommendations
Implementation Fixing issues
