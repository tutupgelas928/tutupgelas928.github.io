Relationships are the most important aspect of most people's lives. They're also the source of the greatest joy and deepest pain. Relationship coaching requires empathy, wisdom, and discretion.

The relationship ladder moves from dating and connection to partnership skills to deep, lasting relationship mastery. Each rung addresses different relationship stages and challenges.

Relationship Stages and Needs

Clients come with different relationship contexts:

  • Singles seeking partnership
  • Daters building connection
  • Committed couples deepening
  • Couples in crisis
  • Long-term partners maintaining

Your ladder should address multiple stages.

Stage Focus
Dating Finding connection
Committed Deepening partnership

Individual or couples coaching. Deep, personalized work on specific relationship challenges. May be short-term intensive or ongoing support.

Retreats and Intensives

Multi-day relationship retreats. Immersive work in a beautiful setting. These create breakthroughs and lasting memories.
