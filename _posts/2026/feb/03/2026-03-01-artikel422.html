You used a fan's leak—great! But if you don't acknowledge them, you break the trust cycle. Here's how to credit and reward your idea-givers so they keep leaking and become your biggest advocates.

  • 🤔 Why credit matters
  • 📢 How to credit
  • 🎁 Reward ideas
  • ⚠️ What to avoid

🤔 Why credit creates a leak culture

When fans see you credit others, they think "my idea could be next." It's social proof that you're a creator who listens. Crediting turns a one-time leaker into a repeat contributor.

📢 3 simple credit methods

  • Tag in the content itself (video caption, photo tag).
  • Verbal shoutout: "Thanks to @user for this idea."
  • Dedicated story: repost their original leak with thanks.

🎁 Reward ideas beyond a tag

  • Exclusive behind-the-scenes access
  • Small gift card or merch
  • Co-create with them: invite them to a live
  • Create a "leaker hall of fame" monthly post

⚠️ Don'ts: stealing vs. borrowing

Never present a leak as your own original idea. Never use without permission if it's a direct image/video. Always DM to ask if you're reposting their content directly.

Remember: A thanked fan is a loyal fan. They'll not only leak again—they'll defend you in comments.
