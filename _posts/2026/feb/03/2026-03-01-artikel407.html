You're tracking leak volume—good. But to optimize, you need deeper analytics. Which leaks perform best? What types drive growth? Here's how to build an analytics dashboard that turns leak data into strategic gold.

  • 📊 Beyond volume
  • 📈 Performance by leak type
  • 👤 Leaker lifetime value
  • ⚡ Leak velocity
  • 📱 Setting up your dashboard

📊 5 metrics beyond raw count
  • Leak conversion rate: % of leaks that become content
  • Time-to-content: Days between leak and publication
  • Leak-to-viral rate: % of leak-content that outperforms average
  • Leaker diversity: Unique leakers vs. repeat
  • Sentiment score: Positive/negative ratio of leaks

📈 Which leak types perform best?

Tag leaks by type and track average views:

Leak typeAvg views (vs baseline)
How-to request+45%
Product idea+22%
Personal story+67%
Correction+33%

This tells you what to prioritize.

👤 Leaker lifetime value (LLV)

Track individual leakers over time. Do they:

  • Keep leaking quality ideas?
  • Engage more after being credited?
  • Become ambassadors?

Calculate LLV = (total engagement from their leaks) / (time). High LLV fans deserve special attention.

⚡ Leak velocity: speed matters

Measure how quickly you act on leaks. Faster action often correlates with better performance. Track average days from leak to publish. Aim for under 7 days for timely topics.

Pro tip: Create a "rapid response" category for leaks you can produce in 24 hours.

📱 Building your analytics dashboard

Use Google Sheets or Airtable with:

  • Pivot tables to summarize by category
  • Charts showing leak volume over time
  • Conditional formatting for high-performers
  • Weekly snapshots to spot trends

Review monthly to refine your co-creation strategy.

Data-driven co-creation: When you measure deeply, you stop guessing. You'll know exactly what leaks to chase and which leakers to nurture. That's the competitive edge.
