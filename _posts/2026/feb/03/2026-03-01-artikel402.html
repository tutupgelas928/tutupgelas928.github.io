🎯 Why brands care about leaks

Brands know that passive audiences don't buy. They want proof that your community:

  • Actively participates (not just scrolls)
  • Generates ideas (shows investment)
  • Trusts your judgment (so they'll trust brand recommendations)

Leaks are evidence of all three.

🏆 Creator who landed a deal with leaks

A small gardening creator (8k followers) pitched a seed company. She included screenshots of 20+ comments asking "what vegetables grow in shade?"—a problem the brand's product solved. She proposed a "co-created shade garden guide." The brand paid $2,500 for the series and promoted it to their audience.

Key: Leaks showed demand before the campaign even existed.

Your secret weapon: While others pitch follower counts, you pitch a community that creates. Brands pay premium for that.
