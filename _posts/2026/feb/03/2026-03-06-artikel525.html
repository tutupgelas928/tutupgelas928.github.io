🎯 Why leak attribution matters
  • Focus: Stop chasing low-impact leaks
  • Proof: Demonstrate co-creation value to brands/stakeholders
  • Optimization: Learn which leak types perform best
  • Motivation: Show leakers their ideas drove results

Attribution turns intuition into data.

🏷️ Essential tracking fields in your database

Add these to your leak repository:

Leak ID: Unique identifier
Content created: Link to resulting content
Publish date: When content went live
Views: Total views
Engagement rate: Likes/comments/shares
Revenue: Direct $ from this content
Attribution score: 1-10 (how directly linked)

Fill these after publishing. Over time, patterns emerge.

📈 Comparing leak vs. non-leak performance

Run regular reports comparing:

  • Average views: leak-based vs. your other content
  • Engagement rate: comments, shares, saves
  • Watch time / retention
  • Conversion rate (if applicable)

Most creators find leak-based content outperforms by 30-200%.

💰 Tracking revenue from leaks

For monetized content:

  • Use UTM parameters on links in leak-based content
  • Create promo codes tied to specific leak campaigns
  • Ask customers: "How did you hear about us?" Include "fan idea" option
  • Track affiliate sales from leak-based reviews

Knowing which leaks generate revenue lets you prioritize profit drivers.

👤 Calculating individual leaker value

Some fans consistently generate high-value ideas. Track:

  • Total views from their ideas
  • Total revenue from their ideas
  • Engagement generated
  • Long-term value (do they keep contributing?)

These "super leakers" deserve special recognition and cultivation.

📱 Building a simple attribution dashboard

Use Google Data Studio, Tableau, or even Excel:

  • Chart: Leak-based content performance over time
  • Table: Top 10 performing leaks (with details)
  • Pie chart: Performance by leak category
  • Leaderboard: Top leakers by impact

Review monthly to guide your leak strategy.

Data-driven co-creation: Attribution turns audience ideas from "nice to have" into measurable assets. Track, learn, and focus on the leaks that actually deliver.
