Not all leaks are created equal. Some are gold; others are "do more of what you do." You can actually teach your audience to leak better. Here's how to level up the quality of ideas your community shares.

  • 📐 Leak framework
  • ✅ Good vs. great
  • 📝 Training content
  • 🏆 Reward quality

📐 Teach the "What + Why" framework

Encourage fans to add "why" to their ideas. Post examples:

  • Weak: "Do a tutorial."
  • Strong: "Do a tutorial on lighting because mine always looks dark."

The "why" gives you context and makes the idea actionable.

✅ Show good vs. great examples

Create a post or story highlighting a great leak you received. Explain why it was helpful. This models the behavior. People learn by imitation.

📝 Create "how to leak" content

Make a short video: "How to suggest ideas I actually use." Share your leak checklist:

  • Is it specific?
  • Does it solve a problem?
  • Can I picture it?

You're literally teaching your audience to be better collaborators.

🏆 Reward high-quality leaks

When someone gives a detailed, creative leak, give them extra recognition. Maybe a "super leaker" badge or monthly spotlight. Quality will rise as fans see what gets rewarded.

Better leaks, better content: Your audience wants to help. Give them the tools, and they'll surprise you with brilliance.
